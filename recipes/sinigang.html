Sinigang

Description

Pork Sinigang is a delicious Filipino sour soup dish. The soup is made from any cut of pork along tomato, string beans, spinach, and tamarind.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups Water
  • 0.5 kg Pork Spareribs
  • 1pc Onion
  • 2pcs Tomato
  • 1pc Finger Chili
  • 0.5 cup Radish
  • 0.5 cup Okra
  • 0.5 cup Sitaw
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sinigang Sampalok With Gabi Mix
  • 1 cup Kangkong

Procedure

  1. Simmer pork with water with onion and tomatoes for 45 minutes.
  2. Add siling panigang, radish, okra and sitaw with 2-min intervals.
  3. Pour MAGGI® Magic Sinigang with Gabi. Stir in kangkong. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve immediately.
